TStar Changelog

Version 1.7.0
-------------
- Improved threading system with per-task cancellation and priority work queue
- Updated continuum subtraction tool
- New tool: multiscale decomposition
- New tool: narrowband normalization
- New tool: narrowband to rgb stars
- Small fixes in the macos installations
- Other minor bugfixes

-------------
- Update to the add/recombine luminance tool

- New small tool: align channels
- Minor bugfixes
