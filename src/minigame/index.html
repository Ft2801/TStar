OBJECTIVE
Survive 15 increasingly brutal waves across 3 sectors. Destroy enemies to earn credits. Between waves, spend credits on upgrades. Reach sector 3 wave 5 and defeat the final boss to win.
ENEMY TYPES
ASTEROIDS — Split into smaller pieces. Watch the debris.  |  DRONES — Fast, homing, fire back.  |  CARRIERS — Slow but spawn mini-drones.  |  MINES — Stationary, explode on proximity.  |  BOSS — End of each sector. Multiple phases.
SCORING
Kills earn credits and score. Combo multiplier increases with consecutive kills — don't get hit or it resets. Collect power-up drops for temporary buffs. Complete bonus objectives each wave for extra credits.
